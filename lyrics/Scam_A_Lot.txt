Satirical Broadway propaganda anthem with theatrical male lead, chanting crowd choruses, and dark carnival march energy.

# SCAM-A-LOT

[THE KING]

I built this kingdom tall and grand!
The finest nation ever seen!
The people love my golden touch —
I make their expectations gleam!
A little tribute here and there,
A simple patriotic fee,
And every loyal citizen
Can dream of living just like me!

[PEASANT CHOIR]

Scam-A-Lot! Scam-A-Lot!
Don't waste your precious time on thought!
Long live the king! Long live the show!
Paint it gold and make it glow!

[THE KING]

Love the symbols! Love the stories!
Love the endless victory!
If the crowd enjoys the spectacle,
What’s the harm in royalty?

[PEASANT CHOIR]

Scam-A-Lot! Scam-A-Lot!
Fame and glory can be bought!
Every debt and tariff scheme
Keeps alive the royal dream!

[THE KING]

The trick is flooding all the zones!
Just keep the chaos spinning!
And if the peasants lose their homes,
Just remind them that we’re winning!

[PEASANT CHOIR]

Scam-A-Lot! Scam-A-Lot!
While you’re sleeping on your cot
Dream of glory for the nation
Ignore the prestidigitation.

[THE KING]

Beware the scholars! Fear the press!
See the hidden plots beneath!
The kingdom must be purified
Through loyalty and true belief!

[PEASANT CHOIR]

Scam-A-Lot! Scam-A-Lot!
What is true and what is not?
Who can we trust? Who should we blame?
Point the finger! Name a name!

[THE KING]

Parades! Crusades! Triumphant marches!
Drums that shake the very earth!
The louder grows the battle cry,
The less they ask what bread is worth!

[PEASANT CHOIR]

Scam-A-Lot! Scam-A-Lot!
Don't think a disloyal thought!
Cheer our soldiers in parades!
Cheer our warriors on Crusades!

[THE KING]

A loaf of bread now costs a crown?
That just proves the market’s strong!
The bridges fell? The schools collapsed?
That proves the old regime was wrong!
The deficit explodes each year?
That makes us prosper as we grow!
If all the numbers look insane,
Ignore the experts; trust the show!

[ALL]

Scam-A-Lot! Scam-A-Lot!
Buy this stock we say is hot!
Keep it moving! Keep it bright!
Everything will work out right!

Scam-A-Lot! Scam-A-Lot!
When the protesters get shot
We will stop investigations
And send the thugs to polling stations.

Scam-A-Lot! Scam-A-Lot!
Legal outcomes can be bought!
Trust the process! Trust the crown!
Surely justice trickles down!

Scam-A-Lot! Scam-A-Lot!
It can never be forgot
Fortune smiles upon the bold,
Fame and glory can be sold!

Scam-A-Lot! Scam-A-Lot!
You can win a big jackpot!
Wave the banners! Beat the drum!
Surely better days will come!
