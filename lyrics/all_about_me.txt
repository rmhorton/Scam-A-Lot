ALL ABOUT ME
(State-media autobiographical operetta for The King)

Satirical Broadway patter song with smug bass-baritone lead vocals, patriotic brass stings, light marching percussion, and dry theatrical delivery. Gilbert & Sullivan-inspired political satire with concise arrangement, sparse instrumentation, and continuous lyrical momentum. Avoid repeated choruses, extended instrumental passages, dramatic reprises, and long transitions between sections. Compact theatrical structure with minimal musical padding.


[CHORUS]

Behold the Man of Destiny!
The Golden Face of Victory!
The Humble Son Prosperity Chose!
The Nation Blooms Wherever He Goes!

[KING]

I never sought importance, friends —
Importance simply followed me!

It's all about me!
It's all about me!
The people love
My humility!

[VERSE: FATHER TAUGHT ME CORRUPTION AND IMAGE]

[KING]

Father taught me business
When I was very small
“The law is just a framework, son —
Connections conquer all!”

Inspectors came inspecting
But our friends came through
And every politician learned
What gratitude can do!

And thus I learned in childhood
What separates the great:
The rules are merely obstacles
That winners renegotiate!

[CHORUS]

Charge Them More! Raise the Price!
Expensive means it must be nice!
If they pay too much, you must be smart!
The higher the price, the greater the art!

[VERSE: GOLF TAUGHT ME TRUTH MANIPULATION]

[KING]

The country club became my school
Where mighty men convene
To celebrate achievement
And admire expensive green!

A gentleman need never cheat
If his people keep the score
And those who count too carefully
Rarely matter anymore!

The caddies learned discretion
No awkward questions reached my ear!
My golf balls wander off somewhere
Then as if by magic reappear!

[CHORUS]

He wisely learned that keeping score
Is the secret of success!
Losers play by rules
Winners just apply finesse!

[VERSE: FAILURE TAUGHT ME BRANDING]

[KING]

Some men are crushed by failure
Their confidence undone
But every grand catastrophe
Is a triumph once it’s spun!

My casinos folded gloriously!
My airline disappeared!
My steaks became collectible!
My shareholders got sheared!

My university folded
My magazines were bland
But every bold disaster simply
Amplified the brand!

[CHORUS]

Put His Name in Lights!
Put His Name in Gold!
Tell the Story Loud and Long
Until the Myth Takes Hold!

[VERSE: POLITICS TAUGHT ME GRIEVANCE: Scandals authenticate the myth]

[KING]

Politics confirmed at last
The lessons of my youth:
The people do not care for facts —
They believe what feels like truth!

They need a mighty champion
To face the enemy
And every accusation proved
How dangerous I must be!

For shame is just a leash they use
To keep the weak in line
And once you transcend consequence
Rules no longer quite apply!

[CHORUS]

No such thing as bad publicity
It draws the press like royalty
If they attack it means you're strong!
Every scandal moves your myth along!

[VERSE: THE GREAT COMEBACK: Transcending institutions, norms, and law]

[KING]

They said the walls were closing in
I was lost, the headlines cried
They said the law would finally win
That history would decide

But every contradiction
Only made the myth more grand!
Normal men may need the truth —
Great men need a brand!

The wealthy gather quietly
The powerful draw near
For men immune to scandal
Are extremely useful here!


[CHORUS]

If they believe, then truth will follow!
Great men shape what crowds will swallow!
Say it loudly! Say it long!
Repetition makes it strong!
Doubt is weakness! Faith is power!
Myth grows stronger every hour!

⸻

[FINAL VERSE — ALL ABOUT ME: Branding replaces reality itself]

[KING]

Some men build a legacy
By serving something higher
I just put my name on everything
And built a great empire!

I learned that facts are fragile
And headlines fade away
But slogans live forever
If repeated every day!

Now children sing my praises
And towers bear my name
And every glorious scandal
Has magnified my fame!

For truth belongs to winners
And history to the loud
And greatness means becoming
The reflection of the crowd!
