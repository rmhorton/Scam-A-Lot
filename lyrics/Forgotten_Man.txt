Dark heartland country-rock anthem set in a veterans bar at closing time. Worn, emotionally sincere male vocal with rough male singalong chorus. Acoustic guitar, warm electric guitar, Hammond organ, restrained drums, steady bass. Starts intimate and lonely, gradually becoming a collective populist movement anthem with subtle militarized rhythm by the finale. Sounds emotionally supportive and patriotic at first, then increasingly authoritarian and cult-like. Themes of loneliness, masculinity, grievance, belonging, propaganda, and algorithmic radicalization. Americana atmosphere, not metal. Tight structure, short intro, minimal instrumental breaks, under 4 minutes.
