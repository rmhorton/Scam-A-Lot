IMPERIAL CASINO
Populism betrayed

Heartland protest rock ballad with emotional male vocals, warm electric guitar, steady drums, subtle organ, and cinematic Americana atmosphere. Intimate verses that build into a powerful anthemic chorus. Earnest, mournful, and humane — not ironic or theatrical. Sounds like late-night pirate radio from a struggling rural town. Mix of Bruce Springsteen, Jason Isbell, Tom Petty, and modern Americana rock. Minimal instrumental breaks, emotionally direct vocals, vivid storytelling.
