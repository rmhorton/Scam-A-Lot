Justice Must Be Blind (Blues Version)


Blues-gospel protest song with Hammond organ, smoky blues guitar, preacher vocals, and call-and-response choir. Southern revival-meeting atmosphere exposing courtroom corruption and hypocrisy. Slow build from solemn testimony to righteous communal outrage. Raw Southern soul and swamp blues energy. Fade out on repeated gospel refrain.
