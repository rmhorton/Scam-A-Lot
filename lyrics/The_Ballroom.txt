Elegant satirical Broadway waltz in 3/4 time. Start sparse and refined with solo piano, light strings, and intimate vocals in a small ballroom atmosphere. Gradually add layers, instruments, chorus voices, brass, timpani, and military snare drums until the song becomes a massive imperial operetta. Increasingly grand, excessive, triumphant, and slightly unstable, like a royal celebration growing beyond control while the kingdom declines. Sweeping orchestration, aristocratic energy, dark undertones beneath the elegance, ending in overwhelming gilded spectacle.

===


THE BALLROOM

[GRAND WALTZ]

⸻

[MASTER OF CEREMONIES]

Welcome, friends of wealth and station!
Welcome to the nation’s crown!
Pay no mind to dust and scaffolds —
Some rather older walls come down…

Observe the subtle renovations!
The aristocratic style and swing!
We hardly changed the White House proper…
Except the tiny eastern wing.

⸻

[EVERYONE]

The Ball-room!
The Ball-room!
At last a hall to fit our king!
With marble floors!
Gilded doors!
The White House is the annex wing!

⸻

[MASTER OF CEREMONIES]

Neighboring kingdoms now avoid us;
Our allies slowly drift away.
The merchants sail past all our harbors…
But keep on dancing! Seize the day!

Beneath us lies a secret fortress
Built to last a thousand years!
With steel blast doors and hidden chambers
To calm the aging monarch’s fears.

…but don’t tell anyone.

⸻

[EVERYONE]

The Ball-room!
The Ball-room!
The crown jewel of democracy!
With golden stairways!
Eagle fountains!
And executive continuity!

…but don’t tell anyone.

⸻

[MASTER OF CEREMONIES, QUIETER]

The old republic once believed in
A certain quiet dignity…
But empires need a grander spectacle —
Now we have Luxury Security!

⸻

[FINAL WALTZ — FASTER]

The debts expand!
The guardrails fall!
But look how splendid is the hall!

Two hundred million
Then four, then eight!
This is how we make America Great!

The music swells!
The couples sway!
Below, the generators hum away!

⸻

[EVERYONE]

The Ball-room!
The Ball-room!
Where history and grandeur meet!

The nation stumbles!
The dancers smile!
His bunker’s forty stories deep!

…but don’t tell anyone.
