ARE WE GREAT YET?

Satirical Broadway propaganda anthem with theatrical call-and-response between a nervous male soloist and a loud patriotic crowd chorus. Starts small and triumphant, then grows progressively bigger, louder, and more unstable with brass fanfares, military drums, chanting crowds, and sweeping orchestration. Grand imperial spectacle, dark humor, authoritarian energy, and increasingly desperate patriotic slogans.

===


[VERSE 1 - PROSPERITY]

[CHOIR]

We’ve made the kingdom Great Again!
Greater than it’s ever been!
Nobody’s ever seen such greatness!
Everybody says we win!

[SOLOIST]

I mean no disrespect, my lords,
But bread grows dear and hard to get…
I’m patriotic as can be…
But … are we great yet?

[CHOIR]

We are Great! Tremendously!
Historic levels of prosperity!
Patriots pay proudly, peasant!
