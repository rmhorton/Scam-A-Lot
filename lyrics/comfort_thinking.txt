COMFORT THINKING

Polished authoritarian wellness-pop. Warm synth pads, soft pulse beat, emotionally soothing vocals. Sounds like a luxury meditation app mixed with subtle political propaganda. Beautiful, hypnotic, sincere, emotionally comforting but psychologically unsettling. Smooth catchy chorus, lush harmonies, gentle electronic textures, calm corporate self-help energy. Intimate vocals, soft reverb, steady calming rhythm. No irony, no hard rock, no aggressive vocals.
